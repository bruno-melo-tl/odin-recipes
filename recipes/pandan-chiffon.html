Pandan Chiffon

Description

Pandan cake is a fluffy, gorgeous green-colored chiffon cake made with aromatic South East Asian pandan paste.

Ingredients

  • 6 eggs
  • ¼ teaspoon cream of tartar
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • 8 tablespoons water
  • ¼ teaspoon pandan paste
  • 5 tablespoons corn oil
  • 1 cup self-rising flour, sifted

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
  2. Combine egg whites and cream of tartar in the bowl of a stand mixer fitted with the whisk attachment; beat egg whites until stiff peaks form.
  3. Beat egg yolks and sugar in a separate bowl until sugar has dissolved.
  4. Fold egg yolk mixture gently into beaten egg whites using a spatula. Pour into a 9-inch chiffon cake mold..
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, 45 to 50 minutes. Invert cake mold onto a cooling rack immediately after removing it from the oven. Let cake cool upside down in the mold. Loosen cooled cake from the mold using a sharp knife.
